Vision

Proper education facilities + dignified healthcare support for the elderly.

Mission

Provide proper learning spaces in remote villages. Education is a human right and the key to improving living standards. The program also targets elderly individuals who rely on family support.

Our Values

Compassionate · Empowering · Trustworthy · Inclusive · Connected & Global-Minded, we respect local culture and work with communities for long-term impact.

Core teams

Meet Our Team

Meet our dedicated team of passionate change-makers who bring expertise, commitment, and heart to our mission. Together, we're building bridges between Germany and Indonesia for sustainable impact in education and elderly care.
